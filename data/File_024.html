2. INCLUSION CRITERIA

  1. Age 50–80 years
  2. MCI due to AD or mild AD dementia (NIA-AA 2018)
  3. Positive amyloid PET (Centiloid ≥25) or CSF Aβ42/40 <0.10
  4. CDR Global Score 0.5 or 1.0
  5. MMSE score 20–30
  6. Stable AD medications ≥3 months
  7. Reliable study partner ≥10h/week
  8. Brain MRI within 6 months — no significant abnormality

4. STATISTICAL ANALYSIS

480 patients (2:1) provide 90% power to detect 0.7-point CDR-SB difference at Week 78 (SD 2.5, two-sided α=0.05, 15% dropout). Primary analysis: MMRM with CDR-SB as dependent variable, treatment, visit, treatment-by-visit, baseline CDR-SB, APOE ε4 status.
