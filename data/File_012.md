## 1. INTRODUCTION

NSCLC represents the most common lung cancer histology. EGFR-activating
mutations drive oncogenesis in ~15% of Western patients. Osimertinib has
transformed first-line management, but resistance is universal after a median
of 18-22 months. BRC-471 was designed to address the most prevalent resistance
mechanisms (HER2 amplification, C797S mutation).

## 5. EFFICACY (EXPANSION COHORT, N=20)

| Endpoint | Result |
|---|---|
| Confirmed ORR (BICR) | 30% (6/20) |
| DCR | 75% |

| HER2-amplified subgroup (n=6) | ORR 83% (5/6) |

## 6. CONCLUSIONS

BRC-471 200 mg BID has a manageable safety profile. PK is predictable and
dose-proportional. Preliminary efficacy encouraging, particularly in HER2-
amplified subgroup. RP2D 200 mg BID selected for Phase II.
