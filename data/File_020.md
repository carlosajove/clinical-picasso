## HEPATIC SAFETY UPDATE

Three cases of ALT elevation >5x ULN (without bilirubin elevation) confirmed
in the Phase III interim (n=189 exposed):
- All 3 resolved within 4-6 weeks of drug hold
- 2 patients resumed at full dose without recurrence
- 1 patient discontinued

**Updated Monitoring Guidance:**
- ALT/AST at Weeks 4, 8, 12, and 24 (in addition to existing schedule)
- Drug held for ALT >5x ULN; Sponsor Medical Monitor notified within 48h
- Rechallenge permitted after resolution to <2x ULN with Sponsor approval

## PEDIATRIC EXPLORATORY DATA

Pilot (n=12, HeFH patients aged 14-17, 75 mg SC q4w, 12 weeks):
- Mean LDL-C reduction: -48% (similar magnitude to adults at lower dose)
- No new safety signals; injection site reactions in 2 patients (mild)
- No pediatric indication is claimed; data presented for context only
