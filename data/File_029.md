## About Biorce Therapeutics

Biorce is a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company based in Madrid,
focused on developing transformative therapies in oncology, neurology,
and cardiology. We currently have three assets in Phase II/III clinical
development and are building a world-class clinical operations team.

## The Role

We are looking for an experienced Clinical Data Manager to join our
growing Data Management team. You will be the data lead on one or more
of our ongoing Phase II/III trials, working closely with biostatistics,
clinical operations, and our EDC vendor (Medidata Rave).

## What You'll Do

- Author and maintain Data Management Plans, edit check specifications,
  and data validation rules
- Manage external data transfers (lab, PK, PRO, ePRO)
- Oversee data cleaning activities and query management
- Participate in database lock and unblinding activities
- Contribute to CDISC CDASH/SDTM mapping and submission readiness
- Support regulatory submissions as required

## What We're Looking For

- 4+ years CDM experience in pharma/biotech/CRO
- Strong expertise with Medidata Rave (study build a plus)
- Knowledge of CDISC standards (CDASH, SDTM)
- Familiarity with ICH E6(R2) GCP requirements
- Fluency in English (Spanish a plus)
- Bachelor's degree in Life Sciences or Computer Science

## What We Offer

- Salary: €55,000–€70,000 depending on experience
- Annual performance bonus up to 15%
- 25 days annual leave + Spanish public holidays
- Remote working flexibility (3 days/week)
- Health insurance (Sanitas) + gym allowance
