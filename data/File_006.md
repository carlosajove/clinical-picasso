# Clinical Study Protocol — AMENDMENT 2

**Sponsor:** Biorce Therapeutics S.L., Calle de Serrano 41, 28001 Madrid, Spain
**Investigational Product:** BRC-471 (Lumitanib) — oral EGFR/HER2 dual inhibitor

## AMENDMENT SUMMARY

This Amendment 2 has been issued to address the following:

1. **Dose modification update** — Addition of prophylactic antiemetic
   (ondansetron) following 3 Grade 3 nausea events in Cycle 1 across sites
2. **Eligibility expansion** — Removal of restriction on prior HER2-targeted
   therapy in early-stage disease
3. **New ctDNA timepoint** — ctDNA collection added at C3D1
4. **Country addition** — Netherlands added (3 sites)
5. **SAP update** — Pre-specified HER2-amplified subgroup analysis added

> Sites must not enrol new patients until local Ethics Committee approval
> of Amendment 2 has been obtained.

## KEY CHANGES

### Section 6.1 — Eligibility (Exclusion Criterion 1 updated)

**PREVIOUS:** Prior treatment with any EGFR/HER2 dual inhibitor OR any
prior HER2-targeted therapy

**NEW:** Prior treatment with any EGFR/HER2 dual inhibitor in the
advanced/metastatic setting. Prior HER2-targeted therapy in the early-stage
or adjuvant setting is permitted if completed >=12 months prior to enrollment.

### Section 8.3 — Nausea Prophylaxis (NEW)

Ondansetron 8 mg PO 30 minutes prior to morning BRC-471 dose, Days 1-7
of Cycle 1. Continue beyond Day 7 at investigator discretion if Grade >=2
nausea persists.

### Schedule of Assessments — ctDNA addition

ctDNA (plasma) now collected at C3D1 in addition to existing timepoints.
