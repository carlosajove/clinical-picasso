[SLIDE 1 -- TITLE]
BRC-471 (Lumitanib) in EGFR-Mutant NSCLC:
Clinical Evidence and Positioning in the Post-Osimertinib Setting

Medical Affairs | Biorce Therapeutics S.L.
Data current as of: 01 December 2023

Speaker note: Remind audience this is an investigational product.
All data from clinical trials. BRC-471 not approved anywhere.

[SLIDE 2 -- THE UNMET NEED]

EGFR-mutant NSCLC: ~15% of Western NSCLC patients
Osimertinib: standard first-line therapy (FLAURA, FLAURA2)
Median PFS with first-line osimertinib: 18-22 months
Resistance: virtually universal

Resistance mechanisms (Park et al. 2022, n=3,218):
  HER2 amplification:   ~12%
  C797S mutation:        ~19%
  MET amplification:     ~15%
  No mechanism found:    ~30%

Key message: NO APPROVED STANDARD OF CARE post-osimertinib.
Historical ORR with chemotherapy: 8-12%.

[SLIDE 3 -- MECHANISM OF ACTION]

BRC-471 is a dual EGFR/HER2 inhibitor targeting the two most
actionable resistance mechanisms.

              EGFR (C797S)    HER2 (amplified)
  IC50:          4.2 nM           6.8 nM
  Selectivity:   >100x kinase panel
  Oral:          BID tablet

Preclinical: 78% TGI in PC9-OR xenograft (30 mg/kg QD)
Not active against MET or KRAS-driven resistance.

Speaker note: Ready for CNS penetration question -- P-gp substrate,
limited BBB crossing; 1 CNS response in Phase I expansion.

[SLIDE 4 -- PHASE I RESULTS]

Phase I BIORCE-ONC-2021-003 (n=42):
  MTD: 250 mg BID | RP2D: 200 mg BID

Safety at RP2D (expansion cohort, n=20):
  Grade >=3 AEs: 35%
  Diarrhoea 71%, rash 62%, nausea 48%
  ILD/pneumonitis: 5% (1 Grade 3, resolved)

Efficacy (post-osimertinib NSCLC, n=20):
  ORR: 30% | DCR: 75%
  HER2-amplified subgroup (n=6): ORR 83%

[SLIDE 5 -- PHASE II INTERIM]

Phase II BIORCE-ONC-2023-001 (n=55 evaluable, data cut Sep 2023):

  Confirmed ORR (BICR): 29.1% (16/55; 95% CI 17.6-42.9)
  DCR: 70.9%
  Median PFS: 5.4 months
  Median DoR: 7.8 months
  HER2-amplified (n=18): ORR 44.4%
  HER2-negative (n=37):  ORR 21.6%

Simon Stage 1 criterion MET. Proceeding to Stage 2 (n=120 total).

[SLIDE 6 -- POSITIONING]

Post-osimertinib landscape:
  1st line: Osimertinib (standard)
  2nd line: NO APPROVED TARGETED THERAPY

BRC-471 potential positioning (investigational):
  2nd line post-osimertinib, especially HER2-amplified
  Biomarker selection: HER2 amp and/or C797S

Speaker note: Do not position as alternative to approved therapy.
Frame as investigational option within clinical trial context only.
