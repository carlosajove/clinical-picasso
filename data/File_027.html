INVESTIGATOR BROCHURE — BRC-209 (VELAGOSTAT)

Selective Tau Aggregation Inhibitor · Oral Tablet · Alzheimer's Disease Program

1. Summary

Phase II Headline Data (BIORCE-NEU-2020-002, n=94, 52 weeks):
Tau PET reduction (entorhinal cortex): −31% vs placebo (p=0.003)
CDR-SB change at 52 weeks: −0.38 points (p=0.08, non-significant at this timepoint)
Safety: No ARIA, no hepatotoxicity, no serious CNS adverse events

3. Preclinical Pharmacology

BRC-209 inhibits heparin-induced tau aggregation IC₅₀ = 180 nM. Binds selectively to tau proline-rich region (Kd 22 nM). No significant activity against 58 kinases at 1 µM including GSK-3β, CDK5, MARK2.

In vivo (PS19 tau transgenic mouse, 40 mg/kg PO QD, 6 months): −44% hippocampal AT8+ tau burden (p<0.01); +18% Morris Water Maze improvement (p<0.05).
