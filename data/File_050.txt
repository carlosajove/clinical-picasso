Edition 3 | Confidential | January 2025

1. SUMMARY OF CHANGES FROM PREVIOUS EDITION

This edition updates the clinical safety and efficacy sections following
completion of enrolment in the Phase III study and availability of the
Week 52 interim analysis data (data cut-off: 30 September 2024, n=412
randomised, n=389 with at least one post-baseline assessment).

Key updates:

  1.1  New hepatic safety signal -- Grade 3 ALT elevation
       Three additional cases of ALT >5x ULN confirmed since Edition 2,
       bringing overall incidence to 2.1% (4/189 exposed in Phase III).
       All cases resolved on drug hold. One patient permanently discontinued;
       three successfully rechallenged.

  1.2  Cardiac safety -- QTcF monitoring clarification
       Pre-specified cardiac safety analysis at 200-patient interim showed
       no QTcF >500ms, no clinically meaningful change in mean QTcF (+3.2ms).
       Existing ECG monitoring schedule considered sufficient.

  1.3  Updated PK -- renal impairment subgroup
       PK sub-study (n=24): AUC increased 38% in moderate renal impairment
       (eGFR 30-44 mL/min). Dose reduction to 100 mg SC q4w now recommended
       for patients with eGFR 30-44 mL/min. Patients with eGFR <30 excluded.

  1.4  Immunogenicity update
       Cumulative ADA incidence in Phase III (n=189): 5.3% (10/189).
       No neutralising antibodies. No PK or safety impact.

  1.5  Paediatric data update
       Pilot (n=12, adolescents 14-17, 75mg SC q4w, 24 weeks): LDL-C -48%,
       sustained. No new safety signals. No paediatric indication claimed.

3. PHASE III INTERIM EFFICACY (Week 52, n=389, data cut 30 Sep 2024)

  LDL-C change:     -56.8% (BRC-883) vs -3.1% (placebo), p<0.0001
  ApoB:             -45.2% vs -1.4% (p<0.0001)
  Non-HDL-C:        -50.7% vs -2.3% (p<0.0001)
  Lp(a):            -22.4% vs +1.8% (p=0.003)
  MACE (52wk):      11 events vs 14 events -- HR 0.76 (NS -- not powered)

  LDL-C targets:
    <1.8 mmol/L Week 52: 66% (BRC-883) vs 3% (placebo)
    <1.4 mmol/L Week 52: 39% vs 1%

4. PHASE III SAFETY (n=412, data cut 30 Sep 2024)

  Any TEAE:              71.4% vs 69.2%
  Grade >=3:              7.3% vs 8.1%
  SAE:                    4.4% vs 5.1%
  Discontinuation:        2.1% vs 1.5%
  Injection site reaction: 9.2% vs 4.1% (all Grade 1-2)
  ALT >5x ULN:            2.1% vs 0% (4 cases -- see 1.1 above)

5. UPDATED HEPATIC MONITORING GUIDANCE

  ALT/AST at: screening, Week 4, 8, 12, 24 (mandatory)
  ALT >3x ULN: repeat within 2 weeks; may continue drug
  ALT >5x ULN: hold drug; notify Sponsor within 48h; repeat weekly
  Rechallenge: permitted after resolution to <2x ULN (Sponsor approval)
  Permanent DC: if ALT >5x ULN recurs on rechallenge

6. BENEFIT-RISK ASSESSMENT

Benefit-risk remains favourable. LDL-C reduction ~57% consistent across
Phase II and Phase III interim. Hepatic signal (ALT >5x ULN, 2.1%)
manageable with enhanced monitoring. No new safety signals of concern.
This document will be updated following Phase III primary analysis (Q3 2025).
