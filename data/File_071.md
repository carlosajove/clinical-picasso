# DATA SAFETY MONITORING BOARD CHARTER
## Study: BIORCE-ONC-2023-001
## BRC-471 (Lumitanib) Phase II Study in EGFR-Mutant NSCLC
### Charter Version: 1.1 | Date: 01 April 2023 | CONFIDENTIAL

---

## 1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

This Charter defines the composition, responsibilities, operating procedures,
and decision-making framework of the independent Data Safety Monitoring Board
(DSMB) established for the Phase II clinical study BIORCE-ONC-2023-001.

The DSMB is an independent committee of experts whose primary responsibility
is to protect the safety and wellbeing of study participants by conducting
periodic reviews of accumulating safety and efficacy data and providing
recommendations to the Sponsor.

This Charter is a binding document governing DSMB operations. It supersedes
all prior communications regarding DSMB procedures for this study.

## 3. DSMB RESPONSIBILITIES

The DSMB shall:

1. Review accumulating safety data at pre-specified intervals (Section 5)
2. Review unblinded interim efficacy data at Stage 1 completion review
3. Assess whether any pre-specified stopping rules have been triggered (Section 6)
4. Advise the Sponsor on continuation, modification, or termination of the study
5. Review any emerging safety signal referred by the Sponsor at any time
6. Maintain strict confidentiality of all unblinded data
7. Provide written recommendations to the Sponsor following each meeting

The DSMB does **not** have authority to modify the protocol directly.
Recommendations to modify the protocol must be implemented by the Sponsor
through the standard protocol amendment process.

- Unblinded treatment-by-arm safety tables (all TEAEs, Grade >=3, SAEs)
- Unblinded interim efficacy data (ORR, DCR, PFS) at Stage 1 review only
- Unblinded narratives for all ILD/pneumonitis cases
- Individual patient narratives for all Grade 4 events and deaths

## 5. MEETING SCHEDULE AND TRIGGERS

| Review # | Trigger | Type | Includes Efficacy? |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 30 patients enrolled | Safety-only | No |
| 2 | 45 patients enrolled | Safety-only | No |
| 3 | 60 patients enrolled (Stage 1 completion) | Safety + Efficacy | Yes — unblinded ORR |
| 4 | 90 patients enrolled | Safety-only | No |
| 5 | Study completion / database lock | Final review | Yes — full unblinded |

**Ad hoc reviews:** The Sponsor Medical Monitor may request an emergency DSMB
review at any time upon identification of a potential safety signal (e.g.,
>=2 new Grade 3 ILD cases within any 30-day period).

### 5.1 Meeting Format

Reviews 1-4: videoconference. Review 5: in person, Madrid. Each meeting:
- **Open session** (Sponsor Medical Monitor present): blinded safety overview
- **Closed session** (DSMB + independent statistician): unblinded review
- **Recommendations session**: DSMB Chair communicates findings to Sponsor

## 6. STOPPING RULES

### 6.1 Safety Stopping Rules

The study will be placed on **clinical hold** pending emergency DSMB review
if **any** of the following occur:

| Event | Threshold |
|---|---|
| Confirmed Grade 4 ILD/pneumonitis | >=1 case |
| QTcF >500 ms (confirmed on repeat ECG) | >=3 cases |
| Drug-related deaths | >=2 cases |
| Unexpected Grade 4 SAE with plausible causal relationship | >=3 cases |

**Note:** Grade 3 ILD alone does not trigger a hold but requires expedited
DSMB notification within 48 hours.

### 6.2 Futility Rule

If, at the Stage 1 completion review (60 patients), confirmed ORR in the first
35 evaluable patients is <8 responses (22.9%), the DSMB will recommend study
termination for futility per the Simon two-stage design.

### 6.3 Efficacy Stopping

Early stopping for overwhelming efficacy is not pre-specified in this Phase II
study. The DSMB may make a non-binding recommendation for accelerated development
if Stage 1 ORR substantially exceeds the threshold.

---

## 7. DECISIONS AND RECOMMENDATIONS

Decisions by simple majority vote. In tie, Chair has casting vote.

Written recommendations to Sponsor within 5 business days of each meeting.
Sponsor acknowledges receipt within 2 business days and responds to any
action-requiring recommendation within 10 business days.

Possible recommendations:
- Continue study as planned
- Continue with enhanced monitoring (specify)
- Implement protocol modification (specify)
- Place study on clinical hold pending further assessment
- Recommend study termination

---

## 8. CONFIDENTIALITY

All DSMB members, independent statistician, and support staff must sign
a confidentiality agreement prior to receiving any study data. Unblinded data
must not be shared with any party outside the closed session.

DSMB members must not trade in Biorce Therapeutics securities while in
possession of material non-public information about the study.
