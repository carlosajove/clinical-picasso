# SUMMARY OF PRODUCT CHARACTERISTICS
## BRC-471 (Lumitanib) 50 mg and 100 mg Film-Coated Tablets

## 1. NAME OF THE MEDICINAL PRODUCT

Lumitanib 50 mg film-coated tablets
Lumitanib 100 mg film-coated tablets

## 2. QUALITATIVE AND QUANTITATIVE COMPOSITION

Each 100 mg tablet contains 100 mg of lumitanib.
Excipient with known effect: lactose monohydrate 45 mg per 100 mg tablet.

## 3. PHARMACEUTICAL FORM

Film-coated tablet. White to off-white, biconvex.
100 mg: Oval, ~14 × 8 mm, debossed "BRC100".

## 4. CLINICAL PARTICULARS

### 4.1 Therapeutic Indications

Lumitanib is indicated as monotherapy for adult patients with locally advanced
or metastatic EGFR-mutant NSCLC (exon 19 deletions or exon 21 L858R) whose
disease has progressed on or after third-generation EGFR TKI therapy.

*[Indication pending EMA approval — to be updated following Phase III data]*

### 4.2 Posology

Recommended dose: **200 mg (two 100 mg tablets) twice daily (BID)**, orally,
approximately 12 hours apart, without planned rest periods.

Dose reductions:
  - First: 150 mg BID
  - Second: 100 mg BID
  - Below 100 mg BID: permanently discontinue

### 4.4 Special Warnings

**ILD/Pneumonitis:** Occurred in ~4% of patients (Grade 3 in ~2%). Monitor
for new or worsening respiratory symptoms. Hold for suspected ILD; discontinue
for confirmed Grade >=3.

**CYP3A4:** Strong inhibitors increase BRC-471 AUC 2.3-fold. Avoid if possible;
if unavoidable, reduce dose to 100 mg BID.

**QTc:** Monitor ECG in patients with risk factors.

### 4.8 Undesirable Effects

Most common (>=20%): diarrhoea (71%), acneiform rash (62%), nausea (48%),
fatigue (45%), paronychia (33%), stomatitis (29%).

## 5. PHARMACOLOGICAL PROPERTIES

### 5.1 Pharmacodynamic Properties

ATC code: L01EB [pending]
Mechanism: Dual EGFR/HER2 TKI. EGFR C797S IC50 4.2 nM; HER2 IC50 6.8 nM.

Phase II (BIORCE-ONC-2023-001, data cut Sep 2023, n=55): ORR 29% (95% CI
17.6-42.9); median PFS 5.4 months.

### 5.2 Pharmacokinetics

| Parameter | Value (200 mg BID, steady state) |
|---|---|
| Tmax (h, median) | 2.1 |
| Cmax,ss (ng/mL) | 892 (CV 31%) |
| AUC0-12h,ss (ng·h/mL) | 18,420 (CV 28%) |
| T½ (h) | 7.3 |
| Primary metabolism | CYP3A4 (major) |
| Protein binding | 94% |

## 6. PHARMACEUTICAL PARTICULARS

Storage: Below 30°C. Keep in original container with desiccant.
Shelf life: 36 months.
