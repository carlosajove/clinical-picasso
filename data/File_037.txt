SECTION 1 -- SUMMARY
----------------------

BRC-883 (Cardanezumab) is a fully human IgG1 monoclonal antibody inhibiting
PCSK9, under development for statin-intolerant HeFH. Administered SC q4w.

Phase II data (BIORCE-CAR-2022-005, n=148, 24 weeks):
  LDL-C reduction: -57.3% vs -2.8% placebo (p<0.0001)
  Safety: well tolerated; injection site reactions 8.1% (mild)

SECTION 2 -- MECHANISM OF ACTION
-----------------------------------

PCSK9 binds LDLR on hepatocytes and promotes lysosomal degradation, reducing
LDLR recycling. Inhibition of PCSK9 by BRC-883 increases LDLR surface density.

BRC-883 binds human PCSK9 with Kd = 0.4 nM (SPR).
No cross-reactivity with PCSK7 or PCSK8.

SECTION 3 -- PHARMACEUTICAL PROPERTIES
