## What Has Changed?

### Additional MRI Scans (First 80 Patients Only)

Following two cases of ARIA-E (Amyloid-Related Imaging Abnormalities —
Edema) in other Alzheimer's studies with similar medicines, we are adding
extra MRI brain scans for the first 80 patients enrolled. These extra
scans will be done at **Week 8** and **Week 18**.

ARIA-E refers to small areas of fluid on the brain visible on MRI.
In most cases it causes no symptoms and resolves on its own when study
medicine is paused. We want to detect it early.

**If ARIA-E is found:** Study medicine will be paused and MRI repeated
in 4 weeks. The study doctor will contact you immediately.

**What this means for you:** 2 extra clinic visits (Week 8 and Week 18)
for MRI scans only. Each visit takes approximately 90 minutes.
