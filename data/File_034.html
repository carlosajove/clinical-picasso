2. Clinical Narrative

A 71-year-old female with EGFR-mutant (exon 19 deletion) Stage IVA NSCLC enrolled in BIORCE-ONC-2023-001 presented on 22 August 2023 (Day 111) with 5-day history of progressive dyspnoea and dry cough. HRCT showed bilateral ground-glass opacities, peripheral distribution, lower lobes bilaterally — consistent with drug-induced pneumonitis. BAL negative for all pathogens. BRC-471 permanently discontinued 24 August. Prednisolone 1 mg/kg initiated 25 August. Hospitalised 14 days (24 Aug–07 Sep). O₂ discontinued 01 Sep. Final HRCT 05 October: near-complete resolution.

3. Causality

Probably related to BRC-471. Temporal relationship, characteristic bilateral GGO pattern, exclusion of infection, improvement on drug hold + steroids.
