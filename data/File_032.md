## ILD/PNEUMONITIS UPDATE

As of September 2023, 4 confirmed cases across Phase I and Phase II
(overall incidence 3.7%, n=108):

| Grade | n | Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| Grade 2 | 2 | Resolved with drug hold + steroids |
| Grade 3 | 2 | 1 resolved; 1 ongoing at data cut |
| Grade 4 | 0 | — |

**Updated guidance:** HRCT required at baseline and at any new respiratory
symptom. Low threshold for drug hold pending radiologic evaluation.

## DRUG-DRUG INTERACTION (NEW)

DDI study (n=12 healthy volunteers): itraconazole 200 mg QD caused 2.3-fold
increase in BRC-471 AUC. **Strong CYP3A4 inhibitors should be avoided.**
If unavoidable, reduce BRC-471 to 100 mg BID.
