Please find enclosed Investigator Brochure Edition 2 for BRC-471 (Lumitanib),
dated 15 October 2023. This edition supersedes Edition 1 (01 December 2022)
and must be read in full before enrolling or treating any further patients.

Edition 2 contains the following material updates which may affect your
assessment of the benefit-risk profile and your conduct of the study:

1. UPDATED PHASE II INTERIM EFFICACY DATA
   Phase II study (n=55 evaluable, data cut 01 September 2023):
   Confirmed ORR 29% (16/55; 95% CI 17.6-42.9) by BICR, exceeding the 25%
   threshold. Median PFS 5.4 months. HER2-amplified subgroup ORR 44%.

2. UPDATED ILD/PNEUMONITIS SIGNAL -- ACTION REQUIRED
   Two additional Grade 3 cases confirmed since Edition 1, total now 4 cases
   (incidence 3.7%). All resolved. IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:

   a) Review updated risk-minimisation guidance in Section 4.3 of IB Edition 2:
      - Baseline HRCT required for all newly enrolled patients
      - Low threshold for drug hold at any new respiratory symptoms

   b) Brief all site staff on updated ILD/pneumonitis guidance

   c) Complete and return the IB Acknowledgement Form within 14 days

3. NEW DRUG-DRUG INTERACTION DATA (CYP3A4)
   DDI study (n=12): 2.3-fold increase in BRC-471 AUC with itraconazole.
   If strong CYP3A4 inhibitor unavoidable: reduce BRC-471 to 100 mg BID.
   Please review all concomitant medications at the next patient visit.
