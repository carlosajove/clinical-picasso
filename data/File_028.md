# CSR-002 — INTERIM CLINICAL STUDY REPORT
## Study: BIORCE-ONC-2023-001
## Phase II Study of BRC-471 in EGFR-Mutant NSCLC Post-Osimertinib
### INTERIM REPORT (Stage 1 Completion) | Date: 15 December 2023 | CONFIDENTIAL

**Data Cut-off:** 01 September 2023
**Patients enrolled at data cut:** 67
**Patients evaluable for efficacy:** 55

## SYNOPSIS

This interim report covers Stage 1 completion of the Simon two-stage design
(55 evaluable patients). The pre-specified Stage 1 criterion was met
(13/35 responses in first 35 evaluable patients, 37%). Updated ORR at this
interim: 29% (16/55), meeting the pre-specified threshold for study success.

## KEY EFFICACY FINDINGS

| Endpoint | Result (data cut 01 Sep 2023) |
|---|---|
| Confirmed ORR (BICR) | 29.1% (16/55; 95% CI 17.6–42.9) |
| DCR | 70.9% (39/55) |
| Median PFS | 5.4 months (95% CI 4.1–7.2) |
| Median DoR | 7.8 months (95% CI 5.1–NR) |
| Ongoing responses at data cut | 9/16 (56%) |

## KEY SAFETY FINDINGS

| Safety Parameter | Result |
|---|---|
| Grade >=3 TEAEs | 38% |
| SAEs | 15% |
| Discontinuations due to AEs | 9% |
| ILD/Pneumonitis (all grades) | 6% (4/67) |
| Fatal AEs (study drug-related) | 0 |

## DSMB REVIEW

DSMB October 2023 review conclusion: Study may continue as planned.
Nausea prophylaxis protocol amendment approved.

## OVERALL CONCLUSION

Stage 1 criterion met. Study proceeds to Stage 2 (enrolment to continue
to 120 patients). Protocol Amendment 2 implemented addressing nausea
prophylaxis, ctDNA timepoint addition, and eligibility expansion.
Full final CSR to be prepared following completion of 24-month follow-up.
