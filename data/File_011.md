# IB-001 — Investigator Brochure
## BRC-471 (Lumitanib) — Edition 1
### Date: 01 December 2022 | CONFIDENTIAL

**Sponsor:** Biorce Therapeutics S.L., Madrid, Spain

## 1. SUMMARY

BRC-471 (Lumitanib) is an orally bioavailable dual inhibitor of EGFR and HER2
tyrosine kinases under investigation for EGFR-mutant advanced NSCLC following
progression on osimertinib.

RP2D of **200 mg BID** established in Phase I (BIORCE-ONC-2021-003, n=42).
Confirmed ORR 22% (9/41 evaluable). Manageable safety profile. DLTs of Grade 3
diarrhoea and Grade 3 rash at 300 mg BID.

## 3. PRECLINICAL PHARMACOLOGY

Potent inhibition: EGFR WT IC50 0.9 nM; EGFR C797S IC50 4.2 nM; HER2 IC50 6.8 nM.
In vivo: 78% TGI in PC9-OR xenograft model at 30 mg/kg PO QD (p<0.001 vs vehicle).
Oral bioavailability (rat): 64%. T½ (rat): 4.2h. P-gp substrate.

## 4. CLINICAL STUDIES

### Phase I (BIORCE-ONC-2021-003, n=42, data cut 15 Nov 2022)

**PK (200 mg BID, steady state):**
  Tmax: 2.1h | AUC0-12h: 18,420 ng·h/mL | T½: 7.3h | Accumulation: 1.9x

**Efficacy (NSCLC expansion, n=41 evaluable):**
  Confirmed ORR: 22% (9 PR) | DCR: 68% | Median DoR: Not reached

**Safety (all dose levels, n=42):**

| Adverse Event | Any Grade (%) | Grade 3+ (%) |
|---|---|---|
| Diarrhoea | 71 | 10 |
| Acneiform rash | 62 | 7 |
| Nausea | 48 | 5 |
| Fatigue | 45 | 2 |
| Paronychia | 33 | 0 |
| Stomatitis | 29 | 2 |
| ALT increase | 17 | 5 |
| ILD/Pneumonitis | 5 | 2 |

DLTs at 300 mg BID: Grade 3 diarrhoea (n=2), Grade 3 rash (n=1)

## 5. BENEFIT-RISK ASSESSMENT

Favourable benefit-risk at 200 mg BID. ORR 22% exceeds historical benchmarks
(~8-12%). Safety consistent with EGFR inhibitor class. Close monitoring for
ILD/pneumonitis warranted.
