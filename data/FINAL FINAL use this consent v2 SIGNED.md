## Introduction

You are being asked to take part in a clinical research study. You have been
diagnosed with EGFR-mutant NSCLC that has progressed on osimertinib.

## Purpose

Testing BRC-471 (Lumitanib), an experimental dual EGFR/HER2 inhibitor. Not
approved anywhere. Aims: shrink cancer, assess duration of benefit, characterise
side effects and pharmacokinetics.

## Duration

Treatment until progression or unacceptable toxicity. Follow-up every 12 weeks
for at least one year after stopping.

## Possible Risks

| Side Effect | How Common | Usually Mild? |
|---|---|---|
| Diarrhoea | ~7 in 10 | Usually |
| Skin rash | ~6 in 10 | Usually |
| Nausea | ~5 in 10 | Usually |
| Fatigue | ~5 in 10 | Usually |
| Lung inflammation (pneumonitis) | ~1 in 20 | Can be serious |
