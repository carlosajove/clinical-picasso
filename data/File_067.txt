===========================================================================
SECTION 1 -- UPDATED EFFICACY DATA (Phase II 18-Month Extension)
===========================================================================

CDR-SB at 18 months:
  BRC-209:  +1.24 (LS mean change from baseline)
  Placebo:  +1.78
  Difference: -0.54 points (p=0.035) -- statistically significant

Tau PET SUVR (temporal composite) at Week 52:
  BRC-209: -28% | Placebo: -4% | p<0.001

Plasma p-tau 181 at Week 52:
  BRC-209: -34% (p=0.001) -- novel finding reported in this edition

Plasma NfL at Week 52: no significant difference (p=0.31) -- exploratory

===========================================================================
SECTION 2 -- CYP2C19 POOR METABOLIZERS
===========================================================================

Pharmacogenomic sub-study (n=18): 4 CYP2C19 poor metabolizers with
2.8-fold higher BRC-209 AUC. Two experienced Grade 2 dizziness.
CYP2C19 genotyping recommended; PM patients start at 200 mg QD.

===========================================================================
SECTION 3 -- FALLS RISK GUIDANCE
===========================================================================

Falls incidence over 18 months: 19% (BRC-209) vs 24% (placebo).
No excess risk attributable to BRC-209. Morse Fall Scale encouraged.
