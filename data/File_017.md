# Clinical Study Protocol — AMENDMENT 1
## BIORCE-NEU-2022-004
### Version 1.1 | Date: 15 February 2023 | CONFIDENTIAL
### SUPERSEDES VERSION 1.0 (03 June 2022)

**Sponsor:** Biorce Therapeutics S.L., Madrid, Spain
**Investigational Product:** BRC-209 (Velagostat) 400 mg QD oral
**Indication:** Early Alzheimer's Disease

## AMENDMENT REASON

Safety-driven update following two cases of asymptomatic ARIA-E
(Amyloid-Related Imaging Abnormalities — Edema) detected on routine MRI,
and investigator request to clarify rescue medication rules.

## CHANGES IMPLEMENTED

### 1. Section 5.2 — New Exclusion Criterion Added

> Patients with any history of ARIA-E or ARIA-H on prior anti-amyloid
> therapy are excluded.

### 2. Section 6 (SoA) — MRI Frequency Increased

Additional MRI scans added at Week 8 and Week 18 for the first 80 patients
enrolled (safety run-in cohort), reviewed by central neuroradiology reader.

If ARIA incidence in run-in cohort is <5%, standard MRI schedule resumes.

### 3. Section 8 — New Safety Guidance

In the event of asymptomatic ARIA-E (Grade 1-2 per ARIA guidelines):
- Study drug must be held
- MRI repeated in 4 weeks
- Resumption requires Sponsor Medical Monitor approval
