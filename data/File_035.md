# ICF-002 — Re-Consent Form
## Study BIORCE-ONC-2023-001 — Amendment 2 Update
### ICF Version 3.0 | Date: 15 December 2023 | CONFIDENTIAL

**Study:** A Phase II Study of BRC-471 (Lumitanib) in Advanced Lung Cancer
**Purpose:** You are already enrolled. This re-consent explains important
changes made to the study that affect you. Please read carefully and sign
to confirm you have been informed and wish to continue.

## What Has Changed?

### Change 1 — Anti-Nausea Medicine Added

We have added ondansetron (an anti-nausea medicine) to be taken 30 minutes
before your morning BRC-471 dose during the first week of each new cycle.
This was added after some patients experienced nausea more troublesome than
expected. The medicine is widely used, well-tolerated, and provided free.

**What this means for you:** Starting at your next clinic visit, you will
be given ondansetron tablets as part of your study supply.

### Change 2 — Additional Blood Sample

We will take an extra small blood sample (~10 mL) at the start of your
third treatment cycle (Day 1 of Cycle 3). This is a "liquid biopsy"
(circulating tumour DNA) to look for early signs of cancer resistance.

## Your Rights Remain Unchanged

Participation continues to be entirely voluntary. You may withdraw at
any time without effect on your medical care.
